About

creator · learner · problem solver

I am a professional musician and former professor of music at a number of Universities throughout Indiana. I am active and respected in the midwest in multiple musical genres, and music will always be central to my life. But by the end of 2020, I knew it was time for a change.

With the encouragement of friends and family, I enrolled in the Eleven Fifty Academy 6 month intensive Web Development boot camp. I have always been eager to learn new things, but this course took that enthusiasm to the next level. Not a day has gone by that saw me out of the code.

As a student in the Eleven Fifty Academy Web Development course, I put to work my strengths and continued building towards my goal of becoming a full stack web developer. What I lack in experience, I more than make up for in aptitude. I have a voracious hunger for knowledge and learning how things work, and I am very excited to continue on the path.
